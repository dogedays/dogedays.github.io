Bike routes

Cruze was developed in order for bicyclists of NYC to have better, safer directions than the random nonsense Google Maps sometimes provides. Instead of taking the shortest route, users are instead routed through safer areas with less construction, traffic, and prioritizes protected bike paths when practical. This app was developed as a team of 3 for HackNYU 2020 and won first place. I originated the app idea and worked on creating big data pipelines and routing algorithms.
